French Toast

Home

Description

A quick, easy, and delicious breakfast meal using mom's homemade recipe.

Ingredients

  • Brioche Bread
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla
  • Butter or cooking oil

Directions

  1. Place skillet on stove and apply oil or butter when it gets hot
  2. Crack some eggs into bowl, pour sugar & vanilla, and mix
  3. Dunk bread into mix and place on skillet
  4. Flip after some time
  5. Serve hot!

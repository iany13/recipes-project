Chipotle Bowl

Home

Description

Nutritious chipotle bowl recipe.

Ingredients

  • Chipotle Seasoned Chicken
  • White Rice
  • Pinto Beans
  • Queso
  • Pico de Gallo
  • Lettuce
  • Vinegrette Dressing

Directions

  1. Cook and season white rice and pinto beans
  2. Cook and season chiptole style rice and beans
  3. Serve rice, then beans, then chicken
  4. Add queso, pico, and lettuce as the toppings
  5. Add vinegrette dressing and enjoy
